## How to Be Listed

Significant contributions in any of these areas may result in being listed as a contributor:

- **Code**: Bug fixes, new features, improvements
- **Hardware**: PCB designs, enclosures, BOM improvements
- **Documentation**: Guides, tutorials, translations, examples
- **Testing**: Bug reports, hardware testing, feedback
- **Community**: Helping others, answering questions
